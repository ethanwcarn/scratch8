My Passion: Vintage Clothing Hunting!

I specialize in looking for old 80's and 90's clothes that fit my stlye. Whether it be from a thrift store or asking people I know, I always love searching for new, exciting pieces of clothing!

  • 1. Here are some of my best sources to find rare clothing:

    • Talking to BYU Alumni and graduating students to see if they have any
    • Designated vintage clothing stores in Provo- Thrifthood, Collective Underground
    • Cheap thrift stores like Goodwill or DI
    • Ebay

    2. Here are some things that I would avoid:

    • Clothes with visible stains, they can be hard to remove and reduce the value of the item
    • Fake leather and worn-out leather- these cannot be restored and get worse with time
    • Clothes that have not been cleaned- have hairs/smell to them. These cause allergies and are a sign of an un-hygienic store
    • Overpriced items. Although some vintage items have a higher price tag, sellers that charge in 200+ ranges are often overpricing by using the 'vintage' adjective.

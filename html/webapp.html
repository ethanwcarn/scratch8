Are They Marriage Material?

Wondering if the person you like is a possible eternal companion? Answer these questions to find out more!

Personal Qualities and Compatibility

Do they have a strong personal testimony of the gospel of Jesus Christ?

Do they consistently live the commandments and honor their covenants?

Are they committed to attending church regularly and participating in temple worship?

Do they display Christlike attributes such as kindness, patience, and humility?

Are they supportive of your spiritual growth and goals?

Do you share similar long-term goals for your family, career, and personal development?

Relationship and Communication

Do they communicate openly and resolve conflicts respectfully?

Do you feel loved, respected, and valued in your relationship?

Do you share similar views on how to balance finances, parenting, and work-life responsibilities?

Are they willing to support you in callings and service opportunities within the Church?

Commitment to Marriage and Family

Are they committed to creating an eternal marriage and family?

Do they honor the law of chastity and encourage you to do the same?

Do they understand and value the principles of partnership, unity, and equal responsibility in marriage?

Are they supportive of raising children in a Christ-centered home?

Do they encourage and support family prayer, scripture study, and other family traditions?

Personal Readiness and Revelation

Have you both prayed about your decision and sought confirmation through the Spirit?

Are you both willing to continue to learn, grow, and change together as part of your eternal journey?

Are you able to be your authentic self around them without fear of judgment?

Do they have a positive and respectful relationship with their own family and friends?

Are you excited about the idea of spending eternity with them and building a family together?
